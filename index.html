Common Questions

Everything you
need to know

How do I apply for the fellowship? +
Simply send your CV to admissions@iisppr.in. Our admissions team reviews applications on a rolling basis and responds within 24 hours. Given the limited 20-seat cohort, we encourage you to apply as early as possible.
Do I need prior research experience? +
No prior research experience is required. The fellowship takes you from the fundamentals all the way through advanced qualitative methods. Whether you're a first-year undergraduate or a working professional, the curriculum meets you where you are.
How does the fieldwork work if it's fully online? +
You'll conduct fieldwork in your own local community — no travel required. IISPPR provides detailed step-by-step instructions for conducting interviews, observations, and community research right where you live. This makes the program accessible to anyone, anywhere in the world.
What happens if I miss a class? +
All session recordings are available for 15 days after each class so you can catch up on anything you miss. There are also designated reserve days after each session specifically designed for reviewing material and completing assignments at your own pace.
Is the certificate internationally recognised? +
IISPPR is a registered organisation in India with a global community of fellows from over 15 countries. The fellowship certificate is recognised in academic and professional circles and can be proudly added to your LinkedIn, CV, and academic portfolio.
What is the book chapter opportunity? +
Outstanding fellows may co-author a book chapter based on their research work. This is optional but highly valuable — a formal publication that strengthens any academic or professional application considerably.
What are the fees? +
The enrollment fee is ₹100 (report writing fee). A convocation fee of ₹1,000 is charged at the end of the second month. Physical certificate courier charges apply and will be communicated after your application is accepted.
